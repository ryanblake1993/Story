Chapter 3 Alternate Ending

No Distractions

Although One ending showed Dylan never really grew up and could not let go of his favourite childhood game. This alternate ending shows a different route for Dylan. He never got attached to “Yu-Gi-Oh”. With this major distraction not ever reaching Dylan he was now fully focused.

Secondary school came and left so fast for Dylan. He was now eighteen years of age and already on his way to College. He applied to study Science. Dylan being the brightest of his group of friends was the only one to attend college that year, the rest followed later. He applied to study Chemistry, Maths and Science were his favourite subjects in school.

Dylan now was excelling in college with no distractions. He was very unlike other people his age in college. He didn’t get distracted as easily. He was always on time for lectures. He would go to bed every night at 10:00pm and he would be fast asleep almost a half hour later. He was focused and did not stray away. He had great potential.

College came and went quickly. Dylan was twenty two years old graduating with a first honours degree in Chemistry Science. His parents, and family came to his graduation and celebrated with a night out together. He was now applying for a master’s degree in Trinity College and was sure to receive it. After meeting his girlfriend Lesley in College they soon prepared to marry. He was on the road to greatness a student who was never distracted. He had great potential. At night he practiced a method he read about online trying to keep his mind awake thinking and not wasting times. He would repeat a mantra to himself quietly “Body asleep, mind awake”. Over and over he practiced this. He eventually had the ability to solve problems while sleeping.
